# SentenSearch

![asd](https://github.com/bugrahankaramollaoglu/SentenSearch/blob/main/graphics/sentensearch_header.png)

### What is it about?
##### SentenSearch is a language learning app where you can access 50,000+ sentences and translations of any word you want in 7 different languages! Here are some of its features:

* You can see translation for every sentence and interface in **7** languages 🇬🇧 🇩🇪 🇫🇷 🇹🇷 🇪🇸 🇷🇺 🇵🇹
* You can export your saved sentences to your device.
* You can listen to pronunciations of every word and sentence.
* You can see synonyms and antonyms of a word.
* You can see your search history.
* Finally, SentenSearch offers dark/light mode for your taste.

### What technologies are used?
* Room database
* Retrofit
* Recycler View
* Programmatical UI
* AdMob
* Shared Preferences
* View Model
